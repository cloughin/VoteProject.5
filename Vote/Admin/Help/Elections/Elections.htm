This form allow you to:
  • select an upcoming or previous election to edit
  • create a new election
  • provide the dates and descriptions of future election that have not yet been created 
When you select an election link you will be provided with a from which will allow you to:
  • identify the office contests in the election
  • identify ballot measures in the election
  • identify the candidates for each election contest
  • enter candidate contact and website information
  • provide candidate biographical and contact information, pictures and candidate positions and views on various issues (only available to certain users)
  • released the election so it can be viewed by the public

Viewing Status: Following the description of each election is the election's viewing status in parenthesis, i.e. . All ballots and reports for any election will NOT be seen by the public until its viewing status is set to indicate that the election is viewable. . This insures that the public will not be able to obtain a ballot or any reports until the election roster is completely entered. Setting of the election viewing status is done on the form presented when you click on an election.
Future Election Dates: Use the election links as examples of dates and descriptions. Any standard date format is acceptable. Dates are to the left of the colon and descriptions are to the right. Do not enter the viewing status which is shown in the parenthesis. You can delete any pair of textboxes that are incorrect or no longer apply. The dates and descriptions described in the Future Election Dates section can be used on the home page to inform voters of future elections.
